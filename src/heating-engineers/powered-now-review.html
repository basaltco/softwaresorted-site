What Powered Now Actually Does

Powered Now is a job management platform built in the UK for UK tradespeople — not a US tool adapted for the British market. That distinction matters for heating engineers because the compliance certificate library was built around UK regulatory requirements from the start rather than retrofitted.

The core workflow covers everything from first enquiry to paid invoice: customer records, quoting, job scheduling, engineer diary management, time tracking, materials recording, compliance certificate production, invoicing, and payment chasing. The mobile app has full feature parity on both Android and iOS — engineers on-site get the same capability as the office view. Everything works offline with automatic sync when connectivity returns.

For heating engineers specifically, the features that distinguish it from the other tools in this category are the compliance certificate library and the annual service management workflow. CP12 Landlord Gas Safety Records, boiler service records, and gas safety certificates are native forms — produced within the app, populated from existing customer and appliance records, signed digitally on-site, and delivered to the landlord automatically. The annual reminder system tracks each certificate expiry date and sends automated reminders to clients ahead of renewal — the core feature for any engineer running a landlord service portfolio.

How It Works on a Real Job

A letting agent manages 40 rental properties and sends you the annual CP12 round in a batch. Here is the Powered Now workflow from that instruction to a signed certificate in the landlord's inbox for each property:

You create the job records in Powered Now and schedule them to the diary — one job per property, all linked to the letting agent's customer record. The appliance details for each property's boiler are already in the system from last year's service visit. Your engineer opens each job on their phone as they work through the round. The app works offline from the moment they leave, which matters when some properties have unreliable connectivity.

At each property, the engineer opens the CP12 form within the job. Customer name, address, appliance details, and your Gas Safe registration number auto-populate from the existing records. The engineer fills in the inspection fields — flue checks, gas pressure readings, ventilation assessment, appliance condition — and captures the tenant's digital signature on-screen. Powered Now generates a formatted CP12 and emails it to the landlord automatically. The job is marked complete, the engineer drives to the next property.

At the end of the round, you raise all 40 invoices from the completed job records in a batch. They push to Xero and payment reminders go out automatically. Powered Now also sets the annual reminder dates against each property — 11 months later, the letting agent gets an automated message that their gas safety checks are coming up for renewal. You get the re-booking without making a single outbound call.

That end-to-end workflow — compliant certificate, automatic delivery, annual recall, invoice, payment chasing — is what Powered Now does that no other general-purpose job management tool in this category does in one system.
