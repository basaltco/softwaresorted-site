Tradify vs Powered Now for Heating Engineers (2026)

This is the comparison most heating engineers end up at. Both tools are widely used by UK Gas Safe engineers. Both are competitively priced. The difference between them — native CP12 production and automated annual reminders versus faster quoting and better Xero sync — maps directly onto two distinct types of heating business. Get this choice right and you will not need to switch later.

Quick Verdict

Powered Now for any heating engineer where CP12 compliance and annual service contracts are the core business. Native CP12 production, automated annual renewal reminders, full offline capability, and CIS handling are all native features that Tradify does not have. At £15/mo for a sole trader, it is also the better-value option.

Tradify for install-led businesses where the daily bottleneck is quoting boiler replacements and heating upgrades quickly. The fastest quoting workflow in the category, the best Xero integration, and the most extensive gas certificate template library. For engineers where new installation work dominates over service contract work, Tradify is the better daily tool.

What Real Users Say

Powered Now users on Capterra who switched from Tradify consistently cite the CP12 workflow: "We were managing CP12s in a separate app before Powered Now. Now it's all in one place and the landlord gets the certificate before we've left the driveway."

Tradify users who considered Powered Now but stayed cite quoting speed: "We price 20 boiler jobs a week. Tradify is just faster for getting quotes out. If we did more annual servicing I'd probably swap, but for installs it's the right tool."

The Xero integration comparison is consistent across G2: "Tradify just syncs. Powered Now works but I have to check it."

Who Should Choose Which

Choose Powered Now if…

  • You produce CP12 certificates as a regular part of your work
  • You manage annual service contracts for landlords or letting agents
  • You want automated annual service renewal reminders
  • You do CIS subcontractor work natively
  • You work in areas with unreliable internet signal
  • Cost is a consideration — it is cheaper at every team size

Choose Tradify if…

  • Your work is primarily new boiler installations and heating upgrades
  • You price many jobs per week and quoting speed matters most
  • Your accountant relies on a clean, automated Xero sync

  • CP12s are occasional rather than a core business workflow

The Split Decision

Some heating engineers genuinely do both — regular new installations with significant install-quoting volume, and an annual service portfolio with CP12 obligations. If this is you, the question is which volume dominates day-to-day admin. Start with Powered Now's trial if the compliance workflow is the current bottleneck. Start with Tradify's trial if quoting speed is the current bottleneck. Both offer 14-day trials with no card required. Running both simultaneously is possible during the trial period if you want a direct comparison on a real job.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is better for Gas Safe heating engineers — Tradify or Powered Now?

Powered Now for any engineer where CP12 production is a regular activity. It is the only tool in this category with native CP12 production and automated annual service reminders. Tradify has a Landlord Gas Safety Record template in its UK forms library, but it requires manual field entry and does not auto-populate from existing records or trigger annual reminders. For install-heavy businesses where CP12s are occasional, Tradify's quoting speed and Xero integration can make it the better daily tool.

Can I use both Tradify and Powered Now?

Running two full systems creates admin duplication and is not practical as a permanent setup. The better approach is to run both 14-day trials simultaneously on real jobs from your business, compare the workflows that matter most to your specific operation, then commit to one. Most heating engineers who have tested both end up choosing Powered Now if CP12 compliance is core, or Tradify if install quoting is the primary bottleneck.

Is Tradify cheaper than Powered Now?

No. Powered Now is cheaper at every team size. Business plan (sole trader): £15/mo vs Tradify Starter at £34/mo. Professional (per user): £25/mo vs Tradify Starter at £34/mo. For a two-person team: £50/mo (Powered Now) vs £68/mo (Tradify). CIS and Making Tax Digital are included in Powered Now Professional; these require Xero configuration in Tradify.
