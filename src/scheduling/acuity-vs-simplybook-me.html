Acuity Scheduling vs SimplyBook.me: Customisation vs Payments (2026)

Of the four tools in this cluster, these two are the closest match for a service business selling more than a single simple appointment type — a therapist with packages, a salon with multiple treatments, a tutor charging per session. The decision between them comes down to where you want the complexity: in the appointment configuration, or in the payment handling.

Quick Verdict

Choose Acuity Scheduling if your priority is selling packages or memberships with detailed custom intake forms and varied appointment types. Choose SimplyBook.me if your priority is taking payment at the point of booking with GBP-native pricing and a stronger default GDPR position — Acuity has no permanent free tier and is US-headquartered; SimplyBook.me is EEA-headquartered (Iceland) with confirmed GBP pricing.

Head-to-Head by Use Case

  • Selling structured packages with detailed intake requirements → Acuity.
  • Taking payment as the primary requirement, GBP pricing matters → SimplyBook.me.
  • GDPR data residency is a priority → SimplyBook.me's EEA headquarters.
  • Want a free tier to test before committing → SimplyBook.me; Acuity has trial only.

The Catches, Side by Side

Acuity's catch: No permanent free tier, and configuring multiple appointment types and intake forms takes longer to set up than SimplyBook.me's payment-first model.

SimplyBook.me's catch: Feature-slot pricing means most businesses need the Standard tier (£20.90/month), not the cheaper Basic tier, once calendar sync, payments, and reminders are enabled.

GDPR Note

SimplyBook.me's EEA (Iceland) headquarters gives it a more straightforward default GDPR position than Acuity, which is US-headquartered and relies on Standard Contractual Clauses. For businesses where customisation is the priority but GDPR data residency also matters, this is a genuine trade-off worth weighing — see our GDPR guide for the full comparison.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is better for selling packages, Acuity or SimplyBook.me?

Acuity Scheduling — package and membership sales with detailed intake forms is one of its core strengths, more specialised than SimplyBook.me's equivalent feature.

Which is cheaper to start with?

SimplyBook.me, since it has a free tier (limited) and confirmed GBP pricing. Acuity has no permanent free tier — only a trial.

Which is more GDPR-friendly?

SimplyBook.me, due to its EEA (Iceland) headquarters, compared to Acuity's US headquarters and reliance on Standard Contractual Clauses.

Which has built-in payment collection?

SimplyBook.me. Acuity supports package and membership payments but is generally considered less specialised at native payment collection than SimplyBook.me.
