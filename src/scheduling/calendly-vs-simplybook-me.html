Calendly vs SimplyBook.me: Simplicity vs Built-In Payments (2026)

The real question between these two isn't which is "better" overall — it's whether you need to take payment at the point of booking. If you do, that single requirement decides the comparison before anything else does.

Quick Verdict

Choose Calendly if you just need clients to book a free slot in your calendar — it's faster to set up and the free tier is genuinely usable. Choose SimplyBook.me if you take payment or deposits at the point of booking, since this is built in natively rather than requiring a separate integration — and it has the added benefit of GBP-native pricing and an EEA (Iceland) headquarters, giving it a stronger default GDPR position.

Head-to-Head by Use Case

  • Simple one-to-one bookings, no payment needed → Calendly.
  • Taking deposits or payment at booking → SimplyBook.me.
  • Want GBP-native pricing with no currency conversion → SimplyBook.me.
  • GDPR data residency is a priority → SimplyBook.me's EEA headquarters gives it the edge.

The Catches, Side by Side

Calendly's catch: Multiple Trustpilot reviewers report UK time zone display issues. No native payment collection.

SimplyBook.me's catch: Feature-slot pricing means most businesses need the Standard tier (£20.90/month) rather than the cheaper Basic tier once calendar sync, payments, and reminders are switched on.

GDPR Note

This is the clearest GDPR distinction in the cluster: Calendly is a US company relying on Standard Contractual Clauses for international transfer, while SimplyBook.me is EEA-headquartered (Iceland) with a more straightforward default position. If GDPR data residency is a deciding factor — particularly for businesses handling sensitive client information — SimplyBook.me has the structural advantage here. Frequently Asked Questions

Which is cheaper, Calendly or SimplyBook.me?

Both have free tiers. Calendly's free tier suits simple bookings with no payment collection; SimplyBook.me's free tier is more limited, but its Standard tier (£20.90/month) is the realistic cost once payments and reminders are needed.

Which is better for taking payments at the point of booking?

SimplyBook.me, by a clear margin — payment collection and invoicing are built into the booking flow itself, while Calendly requires connecting Stripe or PayPal separately.

Which has GBP-native pricing?

SimplyBook.me. Calendly's pricing is USD-denominated and converts with the exchange rate.

Which is more GDPR-friendly?

SimplyBook.me, due to its EEA (Iceland) headquarters. Calendly relies on Standard Contractual Clauses to make US-based data storage lawful for UK and EU customers.
