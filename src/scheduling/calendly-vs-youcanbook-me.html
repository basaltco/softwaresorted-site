Calendly vs YouCanBook.me: Does the UK-Founded Option Win? (2026)

YouCanBook.me's main selling point for a UK audience is that it's a UK-founded company — but once you compare it feature-for-feature against Calendly, the practical differences are smaller than the "UK company" framing suggests.

Quick Verdict

Functionally, Calendly and YouCanBook.me solve the same problem in a similar way — calendar sync, booking pages, automated confirmations. Calendly has a usable free tier and a larger integration ecosystem. YouCanBook.me is UK-founded (though acquired by US company Capacity in 2025) and bills in USD rather than GBP. Unless the UK-founded angle genuinely matters to you on principle, Calendly's free tier makes it the easier starting point.

Head-to-Head by Use Case

  • Want to try for free before committing → Calendly.
  • Prefer a UK-founded vendor on principle → YouCanBook.me, with the caveat that it's now part of a US-owned group.
  • Need the widest integration support → Calendly.
  • Cost predictability matters → Neither is GBP-native, but Calendly's free tier reduces currency exposure if your needs are simple.

The Catches, Side by Side

Calendly's catch: Multiple Trustpilot reviewers report UK time zone display issues — test before relying on it for client-facing meetings.

YouCanBook.me's catch: No GBP-specific pricing despite UK origins, and no clearly differentiated feature set from Calendly. The 2025 acquisition by Capacity is worth knowing about if vendor data-residency history matters to your decision.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is YouCanBook.me actually better for UK businesses than Calendly?

Not on features — the core booking workflow is comparable. The case for YouCanBook.me is about vendor preference (UK-founded) rather than capability, and it doesn't offer GBP-native pricing despite its UK origins.

Which has a free tier, Calendly or YouCanBook.me?

Calendly does. YouCanBook.me offers a free trial only, similar to Acuity Scheduling.

Does either tool bill in GBP?

No — both are USD-denominated. SimplyBook.me is the only tool in this cluster with confirmed GBP pricing.

Is YouCanBook.me still a UK company after being acquired by Capacity?

It remains UK-incorporated, but Capacity (the acquiring company) is US-based. Confirm current data handling and ownership structure directly with YouCanBook.me if this affects your decision.
