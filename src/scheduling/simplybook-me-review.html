If part of your booking process involves taking a deposit or full payment before the appointment happens, Calendly and Acuity will leave you connecting a separate Stripe or PayPal account to do it. SimplyBook.me builds payment collection and invoicing into the booking flow itself — the trade-off is a pricing structure that can climb faster than the advertised entry price suggests.

Quick Verdict

SimplyBook.me is the right choice for UK small businesses that want to take payment at the point of booking — beauticians, tutors, consultants charging for first sessions — without bolting on a separate payment integration. It's also the only tool in this cluster with confirmed GBP-native pricing and EEA (Iceland) headquarters, which gives it a stronger default GDPR posture than the three US-based tools. Budget for the Standard tier (£20.90/month) rather than the cheaper Basic tier once you've switched on the features most businesses actually want — reviewers consistently flag the gap between the advertised entry price and real-world cost.

A Real Workflow Example

A mobile beauty therapist setting up SimplyBook.me: creates service listings for "Manicure" and "Facial" with different durations and prices, switches on online payment collection so clients pay a deposit when booking, connects Google Calendar for availability sync, and sets up automated SMS reminders 24 hours before each appointment. All three of these — payment, calendar sync, SMS — are the features that push the account from Basic into Standard tier, which is worth knowing before you start rather than after the first invoice.

The Catch

Feature-slot pricing. SimplyBook.me prices by "custom feature" — each one you switch on (Google Calendar sync, online payments, SMS reminders) consumes a feature slot, and most businesses will need the Standard tier rather than Basic once these are enabled. Capterra UK reviewers (4.6/5 across 1,281 reviews — the highest review volume of the group) consistently flag this as the gap between the advertised entry price and what they ended up paying. Treat £20.90/month as the realistic starting cost if you want calendar sync, payments, and reminders together, not the £7.50 Basic price.

GDPR and Data Residency

SimplyBook.me is headquartered in Iceland, within the EEA, which gives it a more straightforward GDPR position than Calendly, Acuity, or YouCanBook.me — there's no need for Standard Contractual Clauses to cover a US data transfer in the same way. This makes it a reasonable default if GDPR data residency is a significant factor in your decision, though you should still confirm current data processing terms directly with SimplyBook.me, particularly if you're collecting sensitive client information.
